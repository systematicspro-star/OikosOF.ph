Fresh & Organic Farming

Nestled in the highlands of South Cebu, Oikos Orchard and Farm sits 300 masl, offering breathtaking views of Bohol Island. It is a living testament to sustainable agriculture, biodiversity, and mindful stewardship of the land.

About Our Oikos Orchard & Farm

OIKOS MEANS HOME

Oikos comes from the ancient Greek word οἶκος (oikos), meaning home, household, or dwelling place.

More than just a physical structure, oikos refers to a living system—where people, land, resources, and relationships are interconnected and cared for as one. It is the root of words like ecology (oikos + logos, "study of the home") and economy (oikos + nomos, "management of the home").

For us, Oikos represents a way of thinking: that the farm is not just land to be used, but a home to be nurtured—where nature, community, and future generations belong.

WHO WE ARE

Oikos Orchard & Farm is a regenerative agricultural enterprise and agri-eco-tourism destination located in Sibonga, Cebu, Philippines.

More than a farm, we are a living model of syntropic agroforestry designed to restore soil health, increase biodiversity, and demonstrate how small- to medium-scale farms can become both economically and ecologically sustainable.

The farm integrates food production, ecosystem regeneration, and community engagement, producing high-quality crops while rebuilding natural systems.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We work with nature, not against it.

Our approach prioritizes soil regeneration, biodiversity, and ecological balance over short-term yield. We embrace seasonality and do not force production beyond what the land can sustainably provide.

This means our supply is intentional and limited. It also means our partners understand that farming is not just production—it is a relationship.

Our Offerings

At Oikos Orchard & Farm, what we offer is shaped by the land.
Seasonal, intentional, and grounded in regenerative practice.

Fresh Farm Produce

We grow a diverse range of vegetables, herbs, fruits, edible flowers, and specialty crops using syntropic agroforestry and organic-leaning methods.

Harvested in small batches, guided by seasonality and natural cycles—ensuring quality, traceability, and integrity.

Farm Experiences

We open the farm as a space for learning, immersion, and reconnection with the land and its cycles.

Guided tours, educational visits, and curated experiences that deepen understanding of food systems.

Camping & Glamping

Experience the farm more deeply through overnight stays in nature-immersed accommodations.

Both camping and glamping options allow visitors to rest within a living landscape of trees and biodiversity.

Learning & Partnerships

We host educational groups, interns, and collaborators interested in regenerative agriculture and sustainable systems.

Our farm serves as a living classroom where knowledge is shared through experience and dialogue.
