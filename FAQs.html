Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our farm, services, and bookings

General Questions

You may book a visit by sending us a message through our official social media pages or contacting us directly via our listed mobile/email. Advance booking is required to ensure a guided and well-prepared experience.

We are located in Upper Bae, Sibonga, Cebu. You may navigate via Google Maps or Waze for real-time directions. Detailed directions will also be provided upon confirmed booking. Our address is Vegetable Highway, Upper Bae, Sibonga, Cebu, Philippines.

We understand that plans can change. Bookings may be rescheduled or canceled with prior notice. Full details will be provided upon confirmation, depending on the activity.

Products & Activities

We offer seasonal farm produce such as fresh vegetables, herbs, fruits, edible flowers, and specialty crops grown through regenerative practices. Activities include guided farm tours, learning immersions, farm-to-table experiences, and camping/glamping.

You may purchase our products through:
  • Weekly pop-ups at Banilad Town Center (BTC) and Vibo Place
  • Our retail outlet at Saint Bernard Petron Service Station (Mactan)
  • Direct orders (subject to availability and schedule)

Note: As a regenerative farm, our supply is seasonal and limited.

Certification & Quality

We are currently in the process of pursuing organic certification. In the meantime, we practice regenerative and organic-leaning methods focused on soil health, biodiversity, and chemical-free inputs. We value transparency and welcome visits to see our systems firsthand.

Special Services

Yes, photoshoots are allowed by prior arrangement. We accommodate pre-nuptial, lifestyle, brand, and creative shoots that align with the farm's environment and values. Guidelines and applicable fees will be shared upon inquiry.

We welcome partnerships aligned with our values. You may reach out via our official email or contact our Business Development team through our website or social channels. We look forward to exploring mutually beneficial opportunities with like-minded organizations.
